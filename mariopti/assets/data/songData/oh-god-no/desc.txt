Mario enters Koopas fortress to defeat him once again, but the further in he ventures, strange events and occurences transpire. Feeling uneasy, Mario steps through the final door to encounter... Luigi?                                   He reveals that he has been allied with Koopa for years out of spite for existing in Mario's shadow. Standing high over the heat of a lake of lava, the two brothers fight to the death.
